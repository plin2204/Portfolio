Plotly & Belly Button Biodiversity

Use Plotly.js, a JavaScript data visualization library, to create an interactive bacterial data visualization for each volunteer.
By selecting ID, the web deploys
1. the demographic information panel,
2. a bar chart of the top ten bacterial species in the individual's navel,
3. a gauge chart to plot the weekly washing frequency of the individual, and
4. a bubble chart to visualize the relative frequency of all bacterial species in their belly buttons.
